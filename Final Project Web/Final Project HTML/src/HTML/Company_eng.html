Our Company



The ecoCentric-team has defined ten declarations, which can be categorized as points of reference that are important for us to preserve for all the users of ecoCentric, as well as the usability of our website.


1.Searching for and finding information about our product through the website is easy and straightforward, and it is easy to navigate between the different components.

2.One of our main goals has been to create a website which is functional and available for everybody, so that you get easy access to our information through different platforms.

3.Your privacy is very important to us, and we are never going to use any of your information or your location without your consent.

4.You are in charge when it comes to how much you wish to share with the world through the app, and you can choose to be 100% private if you want to.

5.Our website is designed to be universal and user-friendly, and we have made sure of the fact that our content can be navigated through a screen reader.

6.The colors and the contrasts on our website are carefully chosen, so that it should not be a problem for anyone who is visually impaired.

7.The text-size on our page and in our app is customized so that it is easy to read and understand for everybody.

8.We want to create an ecoCentric-community which includes everybody, and we have zero tolerance for anything that is not contributing to this.

9.Our app is designed to be interesting and available for both children and adults, but our main audience lies with the younger generation – so that we can inspire them to take part in the fight for a better environment and climate.

10.We encourage our users to be a part of co-creating a society that is not afraid of the future, but focuses on the fact that we are making a difference by incorporating greener solutions in our everyday life.
